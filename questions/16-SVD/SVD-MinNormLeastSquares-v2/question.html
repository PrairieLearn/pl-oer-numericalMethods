If $\mathbf{A} = \mathbf{U}\mathbf{\Sigma}\mathbf{V}^T$ is the SVD, then the solution of minimal 2-norm is given by $\mathbf{x} = \mathbf{V}\mathbf{\Sigma}^{+}\mathbf{U}^T\mathbf{b}$

If $\mathbf{\Sigma} = \text{diag}(\sigma_1,\sigma_2,\sigma_3,0)$, then $\mathbf{\Sigma}^{+} = \left(\frac{1}{\sigma_1},\frac{1}{\sigma_2},\frac{1}{\sigma_3},0\right)$
