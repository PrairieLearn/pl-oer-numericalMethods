Consider the matrix ${\bf A}$ with the SVD

$${\bf A}={\bf U}{\bf V}^{T}$$

Given the rank-{{params.rank}} approximation ${\bf A}_{{params.rank}}$ to ${\bf A}$ that minimizes $\|{\bf A}-{\bf A}_{{params.rank}}\|_2$, what is the relative approximation error $ \frac{\displaystyle \|{\bf A}-{\bf A}_{{params.rank}}\|_2}{\displaystyle \| {\bf A}\|_2}$?
