Given a starting guess of $x_0 = {{params.x0}}$, how many steps of Newton's method must you take to approximate the root of $f(x) = {{params.equation}}$ to a residual tolerance of ${{params.tolerance}}$? The residual here is how close the solution is to being a root and is defined as $\left\lvert f(x) \right\rvert$.
