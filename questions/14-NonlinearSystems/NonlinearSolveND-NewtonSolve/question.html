With a starting guess of $ \mathbf{x}^{(0)} = [x_1, x_2]$, $x_1 = {{params.x}}$ and $x_2 = {{params.y}}$, what is the result of the first iteration of Newton's method, solving for $f(x_1, x_2) = (0, 0)$?
