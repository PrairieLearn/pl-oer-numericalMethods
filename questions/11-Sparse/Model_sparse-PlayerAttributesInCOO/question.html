Storing Player Attributes in a COO Matrix

After the end of a soccer season, a company collects the attributes of each player and wants to send them over to a third party for analysis. Before sending the data, the company would like to convert it into a matrix format, such that given a list of attributes ["Acceleration", "Balance", "Jumping", "Stamina", "Strength", "Vision"] a player with {"Acceleration": 92, "Vision": 85, "Stamina": 80} could be represented as $\begin{bmatrix}92 & 0 & 0 & 80 & 0 & 85\end{bmatrix}$, where missing attributes have values of $0$.

In reality, however, there are much more attributes than the ones listed above, whereas the company only knows a few attributes for each player. Therefore to save some storage, the company decides to store the player attribute data in COO matrix format and needs your help to do the conversion.
