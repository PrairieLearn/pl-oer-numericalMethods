Let ${\bf A}$ be an $n\times n$ matrix with full rank and one of the eigenvalues of $({\bf I}-{{params.coe}}{\bf A})$ is ${{params.e1}}$.

Given ${\bf A}^{-1}$ exists, name one of its eigenvalues of ${\bf A}^{-1}$.

Note: ${\bf I}$ is the $n\times n$ identity matrix.

To solve this question you need to understand how to find the eigenvalues of a shifted matrix and eigenvalues of an inverse matrix. See eigenvalues section of the course notes.
