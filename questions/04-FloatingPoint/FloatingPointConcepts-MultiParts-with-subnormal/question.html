For the following problems, consider a floating point number system that can be represented as {{params.representation}} and $b_i \in \{0,1\}$.

a. What is the smallest positive normalized floating point number? Use decimal representation. Enter your answer exactly with no rounding.

b. What is the smallest positive subnormal floating point number?

c. What is the largest positive normalized floating point number? Use decimal representation. Enter your answer exactly with no rounding.

e. How many additional numbers (positive and negative, not including zero) can be represented when using the subnormal representation? Your answer should be an integer.

f. What is the value of machine epsilon for this system? Use decimal representation. Enter your answer exactly with no rounding.
