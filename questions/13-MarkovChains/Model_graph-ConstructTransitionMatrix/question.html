For the given graph, what is the corresponding transition matrix?

The entries of a transition matrix are $a_{ij} = \begin{cases} p_{ij}\quad \text{if }\text{node}_i\leftarrow\text{node}_j\\ 0\quad \text{otherwise}\end{cases}$,
where $p_{ij}$ is the probability that state $j$ will transition to state $i$.
