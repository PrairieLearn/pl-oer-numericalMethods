The error of a method is $E(h) = h^{{params.exp1}}+{{params.coeff2}}h^{{params.exp2}}$, for any $0 < h < 1$. What is the exponent of the tightest asymptotic (big-O) bound on the error, i.e., what is $\alpha$ where the tightest asymptotic bound is $O(h^\alpha)$? Note: The answer is an integer.
